Moving

Use the W,A,S,D (default) (p1) or Arrows keys to move (p2). You will move untill you hit an obstacle.

Stopping

When you go over the stop tile, you will stop.

Bouncing

When you hit someone from you team you bounce them away.

Killing the enemy

You can kill enemies when you hit them from the side.


When you bounce head on you both die.

If you don't move you will always die when you get hit.
